Andrew Romanoff

Former Colorado House speaker, 52

Romanoff, 52, announced Feb. 7. The former House speaker took a progressive stance on a variety of issues in his announcement, including Medicare for All, immigration reform and renewable energy. He also mentioned the Green New Deal to replace fossils fuels that’s being pushed by his former aide, U.S. Rep. Joe Neguse, D-Boulder. Romanoff’s re-entry into politics comes after four years leading Mental Health Colorado, an influential advocacy group in the state. He previously lost a U.S. Senate Democratic primary to Michael Bennet, who now holds the seat, and unsuccessfully challenged then-U.S. Rep.
